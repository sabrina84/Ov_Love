Do you know what PCOS is?

Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome- a name of concern that is increasing at a good rate among women of reproductive age. At least 10-30% of women of reproductive age are suffering this, knowingly or unknowingly. Actually these symptoms are neglected by our women. Most of them consider it normal. And that is the main reason of late presentation. They are bothered only after marriage when they try to get pregnant but can not. It is a non curable disease .Once it happens,it remains forever,but it can be controlled. If it is diagnosed earlier the complications are less.

Be careful about irregular period. It may not be PCOS, but if it is, lifestyle modification is necessary.



Symptoms

The main symptom of PCOS is irregular menstrual cycle. Usually you should have your period within 28 to 35 days. Missing period for more than three months should be taken seriously. It can also indicate you are pregnant, so you better check! If you have less than 9 periods a year, you should certainly see a doctor. ...
